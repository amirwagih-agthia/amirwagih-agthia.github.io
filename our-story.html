Our Story

Al Faysal Bakery & Sweets Co. W.L.L. is a Kuwaiti company established in 1991 under the name of Al Faysal Bakery and Sweets. The company introduced itself into the market with original French-recipe croissants, and mini pizzas.

As the business began to expand, we diversified our product range, introducing new categories of products such as cup cakes, mazmaza / puff and rusks. There was no looking back after this, and the success of the business spoke through the hearts of the consumers.

With over 500 employees and a range of diversified quality products, we are now the most reputed bakery brand in Kuwait, and a household name among our most valued customers.

Our company is versatile and quality-driven, delivering our products daily at specific times to maintain their freshness, through a fleet of over 70 delivery vehicles covering all of Kuwait. Our products are available in all retail food outlets — hypermarkets, supermarkets, cooperative societies, schools, and convenience stores.

During 2014, Kuwait LBO Fund L.P., a Private Equity Fund, acquired a substantial majority stake in Al Faysal with a clear objective of developing and modernizing the production facilities and logistics cycle.
